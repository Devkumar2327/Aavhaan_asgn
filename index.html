What is Aavhan?

Aavhan is IIT Bombay’s annual sports festival, a celebration of sportsmanship, fitness, and competitive spirit.
Since 2017, Aavhan has united 20,000+ students from across India in tournaments, workshops, and online extravaganzas.
Respect All, Fear None.

Why Become a CA?

  • Leadership, event management, and networking
  • Exclusive Aavhan merchandise & rewards
